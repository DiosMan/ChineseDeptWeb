CHIN-121

Second Term Mandarin Chinese (5 Units)

  • Second Term Mandarin Chinese
  • 5 units SC
  • 90 hours lecture
  • Recommended: CHIN-120 or equivalent

A continuation of CHIN-120 for verbal and written purposes. Use of original Chinese characters is introduced at the sentence and the paragraph level. Students will be familiarized with both simplified and original writing systems. Cultural topics may include education, family, and daily life. Writing skills will be emphasized. The proficiency level should develop to a basic survival level. CSU, UC


  1. Create a Chinese business card.
  2. Write a standard note.
  3. Discuss complex aspects of Chinese culture and tradition.
  4. Combine Chinese pronunciation and complicated words to make complex statements.
  5. Comprehension more complex grammatical structures.
  6. Contextualization of vocabulary and idioms.
  7. Improve comprehension of detailed conversation.
  8. Write compositions using Pinyin system and Chinese complicated words appropriately.
