Step 1: click <Start> and <My Computer>.

Step 2: click <Control Panel>.

Step 3: click <Regional and Language Options>.

Step 4: click <Language>.

Step 5: click <Details...>.

Step 6: Check on the following window. If you can't find Chinese (RPC) or Chinese (Taiwan), go to Step 7.

             If you find it, go to Step 13.

Step 7: click <Add>.

Step 8: Find the one that you want to use and click on it.

Step 9: If you are using Chinese (PRC). Please find <Chinese (Simplified) - Microsft Pinyin IME 3.0>.

            If you are using Chinese (Taiwan). Please find <Microsoft New Phonetic IME 2002a>. 

Step 10: click <Apply> if you can see it on the following window.

Step 11: click on <Language Bar...>.

Step 12: Make sure those are being checked, and click <OK>.

Step 13: Change the one that you want to use at right hand side corner.
